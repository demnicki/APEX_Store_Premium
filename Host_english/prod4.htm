"Hotel reception" application, first of all, is designed to help employees of a company providing hotel and catering services. It has sub-pages for the receptionist, or hotel manager, for recording available rooms for guests. And also for managing reservations for these rooms. In this we can perform thanks to this application, such processes as: adding, editing and deleting reservations. Checking the status of payments.

This application is fully integrated with the interface of "Booking.com", so that your customer's data goes from "Booking.com", directly to the Oracle database. And thanks to our application, you can generate relevant reports right away.

Also, the app has sub-pages for managing reservations for tables in the restaurant, dedicated to the head waiter.

This application is available in English, and Arabic.
