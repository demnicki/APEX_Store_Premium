Through our "APEX Store Premium" exchange, you can also lease cloud computing on Oracle's Oracle Cloud Infrastructure service Corporation. And that's without having to register an account with Oracle.com. With the button "plus" or "minus", you can adjust the monthly subscription, which is 120 euros gross, for 20 gigabytes of disk space. Additional components, such as the Oracle Enterprise 23ai database, or the APEX platform - is completely free. For increasing the space by another 20 gigabytes, you simply pay a multiple of the basic subscription.

This is a good solution for a business customer who is just testing a cloud solution. However, our team recommends that you use Oracle Cloud Infrastructure, directly on Oracle.com.
