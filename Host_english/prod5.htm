APEX platform application Warehouse manager

Studio APEX4biz.com 4.98 euros

This is an application, to manage warehouse management, including the creation of records of goods, with the determination of width, length, height, net weight and gross weight. So as to correctly predict how quickly the warehouse will be filled, while knowing the rate of delivery of individual goods. The application calculates how much storage space is left, while recording orders of goods coming in and going out of the warehouse.

This application, also allows the creation of separate storage categories. For example, it can create separate categories for products that require freezing - storage below 0 degrees Celsius, or flammable and explosive, or fragile like glass or porcelain.

It is an ideal application, for a warehouse manager or a procurement officer. This application is available in Polish, English, and Arabic.
